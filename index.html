What I do

how did I get here?

  • a little while ago...

    I was born in the beautiful beach of Tampico, Mexico, where I grew up surrounded my family and friends. I was always interested in maths, arts, creative stuff, and dogs :)

  • 2005

    Hurray! I am an engineer!

    I achieved a BEng Mechanical Administrative Engineering degree from Universidad the Monterrey in Mexico. I graduated 1st in my class with Cum Laude Honours.

  • 2006-2009

    Business, business, business

    I worked in Exiros, the procument company of the Tenaris Techint Group, in Monterrey, Mexico. I evaluated user's needs, analysed technical and economic specifications of bids, and awarded suppliers contracts, managed the implementation of contracts ensuring a correct and smooth starting curve and progress of each project.

  • 2009-2010

    Learning that life is more than business

    Because I wanted to learn about sustainable practices and community led projects, I worked as a Jesuit Volunteer at the rural cooperative Comunidades Campesinas en Camino in Oaxaca, Mexico, which is based on organic and fair-trade production from indigenous groups. I led the export team, developed economic evaluation, strategic programming, and production strategies.

  • 2010-2011

    Pursuing my love for art and sustainability

    I was awarded with a scholarship from the governemnt of Mexico (CONACYT) to come to London to study an MA in Design and Environment from Goldsmiths College, where I graduated with a Merit.

  • 2013-now

    Engineer + Sustainability + Community + Art

    Again I was awarded with a scholarishop to study a PhD in Civil, Environmental, and Geomatic Engineering at University College London.
